About Coffee Crisp

Coffee Crisp is a delightful chocolate bar made in Canada. Introduced in 1938, it features layers of vanilla wafer and a foamed coffee-flavored soft candy, all covered with a milk chocolate outer layer. Originally created by Rowntree's, it is now proudly owned by Nestlé.

Learn more about the history of Coffee Crisp.

Variations

Experience the diversity of Coffee Crisp with various flavors introduced over the years. From Coffee Crisp Orange to Café Caramel, each variation brings a unique twist to this beloved Canadian treat.
